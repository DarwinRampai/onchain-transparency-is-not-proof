## Abstract

This working paper reports twenty bounded Aave V3 Ethereum experiments showing why public event history, exact-block state, derived measurements, causal explanation, historical action, and authority provenance must remain distinct evidence classes.

## Scope

The empirical specimen is bounded to Aave V3 Ethereum at block 20,000,000. The work does not allege protocol wrongdoing, provide trading signals, produce execution logic, or claim market-wide frequencies.

## Decision Evidence Reviews

The public paper demonstrates financial evidence reconstruction on a bounded DeFi lending specimen. A private Decision Evidence Review applies the method to one consequential financial decision and up to five accepted claims.

The review separates public assertions, controlled evidence, current state, historical action, authorization, provenance, reproducibility, Unknowns, and contradictions. It does not sell a predetermined conclusion, rank investments, or provide trading instructions.
