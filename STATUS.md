## Current Determination

The public record demonstrates, for a bounded Aave V3 Ethereum specimen at
block 20,000,000, that event history, exact-block state, derived measurement,
causal explanation, historical configuration action, and authority provenance
are distinct evidence classes.

The record does not establish market-wide frequencies, protocol wrongdoing,
investment conclusions, trading signals, or execution instructions.

## Evidence Boundary

- The paper is the canonical narrative statement.
- The `evidence/` directory is the public reproducibility record.

- GitHub records public corrections and later evidence changes.
- Private or controlled evidence is not represented as publicly verified.
